About Cambrian

Education is an essential tool for the development of any society, and Nigeria is no exception. However, many children in the North-Central part of Nigeria face significant barriers to accessing quality education. These are often children who were abandoned by their family and left to roam about the street and they are not considered a priority by the wider society.
